Privacy Policy

The QuickMarks for YouTube extension respects the privacy and security of its users' data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the data collected by the extension.

Data Collected

The extension does not collect or store any personal or sensitive data. All data generated is limited to video bookmarks and stored locally in the user's browser. This data includes:

  • Video timestamp (bookmark time).
  • Video title.
  • Thumbnails automatically generated for each bookmark.

How We Use the Data

The data is used exclusively to allow users to save and access their favorite moments in YouTube videos. This data is stored locally and is not sent to any external server.

Data Sharing

The QuickMarks for YouTube extension does not share data with third parties. No personal or identifiable information is collected or stored.

Security

All data managed by the extension is securely stored in the user's browser, and no information is shared outside of this environment. User privacy is our priority.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. If there are significant changes, we will notify users through the extension or by updating the available documentation.
